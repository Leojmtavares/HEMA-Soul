About
HEMA Soul
Welcome to HEMA Soul, where history comes alive through the art of Historical European Martial Arts (HEMA). Our passion lies in preserving and practicing the martial traditions of Europe, rooted in authentic techniques from centuries-old manuscripts. We are a dedicated team of historians, martial artists, and enthusiasts who strive to bring the past into the present. Whether you're here to explore the craft of swordsmanship, discover the discipline of polearms, or experience the elegance of the rapier, we offer a welcoming environment for students of all skill levels.

At HEMA Soul, our mission is to:
Preserve History:
By studying and interpreting historical combat manuals, we ensure these traditions are not lost to time. 		Build Community:
We believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where people can learn, grow, and connect through their shared love for HEMA.

 Promote Excellence:
From workshops to tournaments, we aim to equip our members with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in this unique martial art.
Whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned practitioner, we invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Together, we'll uncover the techniques, discipline, and artistry that defined European martial traditions—and make history a living, breathing part of today.
Discover your inner swordsman. Step into the world of HEMA with us.
