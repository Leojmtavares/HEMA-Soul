RULES


1. Respect All Members
Treat everyone—students, instructors, and visitors—with courtesy and kindness.
2. Practice Safety First
Always wear appropriate protective gear during training and sparring.

Follow instructor guidance to minimize risks and ensure everyone’s safety.

Report any injuries or unsafe practices immediately to the instructor.
3. Honor the Art

Approach HEMA with a mindset of learning and respect for its historical roots.

Strive for authenticity and excellence in your practice, while maintaining a humble attitude.

4. Foster a Collaborative Spirit
Share knowledge and support the growth of fellow members, regardless of skill level.

Avoid overly competitive behavior during training; we’re here to learn together.
5. Maintain Equipment and Space
Take care of personal and shared equipment, ensuring it’s in safe, working condition.

Keep training spaces clean and organized.
6. Communicate Openly
Voice concerns, suggestions, or feedback respectfully to the instructors or management.

If conflicts arise, address them constructively and privately.
7. Represent the Community Positively
Uphold the values of HEMA Soul both inside and outside the training hall.

Be an ambassador for the art and our community at events and online.
